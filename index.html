Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies dot com!!!

How it all Started:

Our company was founded one rainy day in March when our CEO Pat accidentally used salmon paste in his cookie instead of chocolate chips. And the rest? History.

Locations:

We sell delicious salmon-flavored cookies all across the greater Seattle region. We currently have stores in downtown Seattle, SeaTac airport, Capitol Hill, and Alki beach!

Child Labor:

We are firm believers in a child's right to be gainfully employed. Our cookies are made with all of the love and wonder that only kids truly have, and we know that it shows in the taste. Ethical child labor is one of the core principles of our business model.

Merch Store:

Check out our merchandise! We are currently selling salmon cookie T-shirts and plushies. Keep an eye out for more to come!

Business Hours:

All stores are open from 8pm to 6am on weekdays, and 24 hours a day on weekends!
